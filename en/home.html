LES-EXPO 2025

Freight Engineering Services: The Strategic Discipline of Heavy Transportation

Load engineering is a multidisciplinary field that involves the safe, accurate and planned lifting, transportation and placement of loads of unusual size and weight. This process is not only realized with vehicles, but also with the planning, analysis and decisions of the expert engineers who manage these vehicles.

Although not formally defined, load engineering encompasses critical processes such as risk analysis, ground assessment, vehicle selection, and application monitoring in heavy-duty transport operations by land, air, sea, rail, and sometimes even undersea.

With the change of technology, climate and projects, the variety of cargo transported is increasing, making the future role of cargo engineering specialists even more vital.

Reserve Your Place

The future of freight engineering takes shape at les-expo.

LES-EXPO 2025 brings together the latest developments, technologies and innovative solutions in the cargo engineering sector. With the participation of expert companies and professionals in the field, this fair offers a unique opportunity to discover the latest trends in the sector and revolutionary innovations in engineering.

LES-EXPO, the center of innovations, technological breakthroughs and strategic partnerships in the field of load engineering, is an international fair that brings together all stakeholders in the sector under one roof. Exhibiting a wide range of products and services from heavy-lifting systems to transportation solutions, from structural support technologies to digital planning tools, LES-EXPO is one of the gatherings that shape the world of engineering. Throughout the fair, leading companies in the sector exhibit their latest products and projects, while visitors have the opportunity to follow current trends on site, experience technologies with practical demonstrations, and communicate directly with experts in their fields.

LES-EXPO is not only an exhibition area, but also a dynamic platform for sharing knowledge, transferring experience and establishing professional networks. This event, full of seminars, technical sessions and special events, offers an inspiring environment for both professionals looking for technical depth and those new to the sector.
